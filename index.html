About Me.

I’m Rayyan Siddiqui, a 19-year-old Software Engineer at Microsoft, the youngest Microsoft engineer in history. I’ve been pushing boundaries from a young age:

  • At age 9, I wrote my first line of code.
  • At age 12, I built the largest anime streaming app in the world.
  • At age 13, I became the Youngest Certified Android App Developer, recognized by Google, and the Youngest Udacity Nanodegree Graduate.
  • At age 14, I graduated high school with honors.
  • At age 15, I became the youngest student in history to attend the University of British Columbia.
  • At age 16, I joined Microsoft as a Software Engineer.

I’m the co-founder of RayTech, a leading app development company which has collaborated with some of the biggest brands in the world. Learn More
